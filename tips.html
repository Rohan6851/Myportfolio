How to make your PC/Laptop Welcome You at startup

step 1- first of all open Notepad and paste tha following code-
Dim speaks, speech
speaks="Welcome Rohan Boss"
Set speech=CreateObject("sapi.spvoice")
speech.Speak speaks
step 2- save notepad file with (.vbs) extension
step 3- press windows+R and type shell:startup and press enter
step 4- and paste the .vbs in it and restart your pc

How to play PUBG Mobile on PC without using Emulator??

Follow these steps :-
step 1. Download and Install (APowerSoft) in your PC/Laptop to mirror your phone.
step 2. Enable USB Debugging in your device by navigating to phone settings.
step 3. Download (APowerMirror) app from Google Play store in your Android device.
step 4. Connect both ends of the USB data cable to phone and PC respectively.
step 5. Mirror the screen and open PUBG Mobile game on the PC.
step 6. Configure all the Keymap settings, and the game is ready to play.

How to Enable windows 10 restore point-

step 1. Open Start.
step 2. Search for Create a restore point, and click the top result to open the System Properties experience.
step 3. Under the "Protection Settings" section, select the main "System" drive, and click the Configure button.
step 4. Select the Turn on system protection option.
step 5. Select disk space (under uses)
step 6. Click the Apply button.
step 7. Click the OK button.
